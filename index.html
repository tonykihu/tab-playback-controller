Features

Everything You Need to
Tame Browser Audio

Free core features that work out of the box. No account needed.

Smart Detection

Automatically finds active media in your tabs. Filters out hidden or inactive players — no false positives.

Play / Pause Control

Toggle playback per-tab or all at once. One-click Pause All when you need instant silence.

Domain Grouping

Multiple YouTube tabs? They're grouped together automatically. Collapse or expand each group.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Alt+Shift+M to toggle, Alt+Shift+P to pause all. Works globally — no popup needed.

Lightweight

Event-driven service worker. Zero polling, zero background CPU. Wakes only when a tab changes.

Privacy First

No data collection. No accounts. No tracking. Everything runs locally in your browser.
